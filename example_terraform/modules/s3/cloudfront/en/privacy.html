Personal information is destroyed without delay when the purpose of collection and use is achieved. However, for the following information, the information is retained for the specified period even after membership withdrawal for the following reasons.

1) Reasons for information retention according to company internal policy

A. Misuse Records
- Items to be preserved: records of illegal use (records of abnormal service use, such as records of illegal subscriptions and violations of regulations)
- Reason for retention: Prevention of illegal subscription and illegal use지

B. Prevention of illegal use by members and prevention of unauthorized use
- Preservation items: chat history (data storage for prevention of various criminal acts and cooperation in investigation)

- Retention period: 10 days

C. Information to prevent repeated withdrawal/re-registration

Personal information included in the member information of users who do not use the service for a long time is separately stored. If you want to reuse the service, you can restore the normal ID by selecting the dormant cancellation process.
- Separate storage items: Personal information included in member information (including mandatory and optional information)

- Reason for separate storage: Protection of personal information of long-term non-users according to 「Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, Etc.」

6. Procedure and method of destruction of personal information
In principle, the personal information of users is destroyed without delay after the purpose of collection and use of personal information is achieved. The destruction procedure and method are as follows.

1) Destruction procedure
The information entered by the user for membership registration, etc. is moved to a separate DB after the purpose is achieved (a separate filing cabinet in the case of paper) and is scheduled according to internal policies and other information protection reasons according to relevant laws (refer to retention and use period) It is destroyed after being stored for a period of time. This personal information will not be used for any other purpose except as required by law.

2) Criteria for destruction
- Request to withdraw from membership by the user or his/her legal representative
- Request to withdraw from membership through personal information-related organizations such as Korea Internet & Security Agency and identity verification agencies
- Request to withdraw consent to collection and use of personal information and to delete or destroy personal information
- Long-term non-users according to the Information and Communications Network Act

3) Destruction method
- Personal information stored in the form of an electronic file is deleted using a technical method that cannot reproduce the record.
- Personal information printed on paper is shredded with a shredder or destroyed through incineration.

8. Technical/Administrative Protection Measures for Personal Information

The company is taking the following technical/administrative measures to ensure safety so that personal information is not lost, stolen, leaked, altered or damaged in processing members' personal information.

1) Password encryption
The password of the ID is encrypted, stored and managed, so only the person knows, and only the person who knows the password can check and change personal information. The encryption uses a hashing method by implementing a multi-fragment encryption using bcrypt.

2) Countermeasures against hacking
The company is doing its best to prevent leakage or damage to users' personal information by hacking or computer viruses. Data are frequently backed up in preparation for damage to personal information, and users' personal information or data is prevented from being leaked or damaged by using cloud security services. are making it happen. In addition, an intrusion prevention system is used to control unauthorized access from the outside, and we are trying to equip all possible technical devices to secure security systematically.

3) Minimization and training of handling staff
The company's personal information processing staff is limited to the person in charge, and the account that can view detailed personal information has been segregated and security measures have been taken. A separate password for this account is regularly renewed through procedures, and compliance with the privacy policy is always emphasized through frequent training for the person in charge.

4) Operation of a private information protection organization
Through the in-house personal information protection organization, etc., we check the implementation of the personal information processing policy and the compliance of the person in charge, so that if a problem is found, it is immediately corrected and rectified. However, the company is not responsible for any problems caused by the leakage of personal information such as ID and password due to the member's negligence or problems on the Internet.
